With a track record of building scalable systems, he specializes in **LLM-driven solutions** for chat, search, fraud detection, and agent assistance. He has led high-impact projects like the **Coinbase Chatbot** (automating ~50% of contacts) and **Bad Actor Guardrails** (preventing fraud with >99% precision).

Previously, as a Founding Machine Learning Scientist at **Agara** (acquired by Coinbase), he pioneered AI-driven voice automation and real-time conversation intelligence. His early work at **Sigtuple** involved developing deep learning models for medical imaging analysis.

Rahul holds a Master's in Computer Science from UIUC and patents in text-to-SQL generation and customer interaction summarization. He is passionate about mentoring teams and pushing the boundaries of **Agentic AI**.
