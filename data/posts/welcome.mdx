## The Roots: Research & Audio Forensics

Before diving into the industry, my passion for technology was rooted in research.

## The Early Days: Computer Vision & Medical Imaging

My professional journey began at **Sigtuple**, where I worked on **Aadi**, a computer vision-based analyzer for andrology.

- I leveraged Deep Learning (UNet, ResNets) for morphological analysis.
- I solved complex multi-object tracking problems to analyze medical samples.

This experience taught me the rigor required to build AI systems where precision matters.

## The Voice AI Revolution: Agara

I then joined **Agara** as a Founding Machine Learning Scientist. Here, I pioneered **AI-driven voice automation**.

- We built **Voice Assist** to help human agents in real-time.
- We developed **Voice Auto**, an autonomous agent with state-of-the-art Text-to-Speech capabilities.

Agara was eventually acquired by **Coinbase**, marking the next chapter of my journey.

## The Era of GenAI: Coinbase

At **Coinbase**, I lead the development of **LLM-powered systems** that transform customer experience and security.

- **Chatbot**: Built the first in-house LLM chatbot automating ~50% of support contacts.
- **Security**: Developed "Bad Actor Guardrails" to detect fraud with >99% precision.
- **Agentic AI**: Now, I focus on building **Multi-Agent Systems** that can reason, plan, and execute tasks autonomously.
